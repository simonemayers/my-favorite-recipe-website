Ingredients to make Homemade Vegan Pan Pizza

  • For The Base
    • 135g all-purpose flour + more for dusting
    • 100 ml lukewarm water
    • 7g of sugar
    • 4g of salt
    • 5g instant yeast
    • Olive oil, for greasing
    • 100 ml spicy marinara
  • Toppings
    • 1/4 cup onions, bite sized pieces
    • 1/2 cup assorted colored bell peppers, sliced
    • 1/2 cup spinach leaves
    • 1 tsp dried red pepper flakes
